FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear the most.

General

What kind of clinics is Nevic built for?
We build softwares for every sized clinics and hospitals. It is designed for practices without a dedicated IT team.
Do I need to replace my existing HMS?
No. Nevic Labs products works alongside your current setup and becomes the operational source of truth for OPD flow, appointments, and patient communication.
Which languages are supported?
Currently English, Hindi and Marathi across all patient-facing touchpoints, with staff interfaces in English.
How long does onboarding take?
A single-doctor OPD can be live in days. Multi-doctor setups take a little longer to configure roles and queues.
Does it work on low-end devices and patchy internet?
Yes. The admin app is lightweight and the WhatsApp-native flows run on any basic smartphone.
Can multiple doctors and receptionists use it at once?
Yes, with role-based access so each person sees only what is relevant to their role.
What if my staff isn't tech-savvy?
The interfaces are deliberately simple, and most patient interaction happens over WhatsApp, which staff and patients already know. We also provide dedicated support post setup.
Is there per-doctor customization?
Yes. Queues, availability, and views can be configured per doctor without any custom code.
What support do you provide after go-live?
Onboarding help, configuration support, and issue resolution during your pilot and beyond.
Can I start small and expand later?
Yes. Most clinics begin with one or two OPD doctors and add modules and locations over time.

Nevic Pulse

What does Nevic Pulse do?
It manages OPD and clinic operations — appointments, patient queues, and day-to-day front-desk workflow in one place.
How does appointment booking work?
Bookings are done in real time over whatsapp and each is tagged by source (walk-in or WhatsApp) to keep queues realistic.
Can I see a live patient queue?
Yes. A real-time queue with per-doctor toggle views lets each doctor see only their own patients.
What roles are supported?
Receptionist, doctor, and admin/owner — each with a tailored view and permissions.
Does Nevic Pulse handle billing?
Yes, Nevic Pulse focuses on OPD operations and covers the billings.
Can the owner see clinic-wide activity?
Yes. The admin/owner view gives visibility across doctors, appointments, and daily flow.
Does it integrate with my current HMS?
If your existing HMS company provides the APIs, we can easily integrate the two.
Can walk-ins and WhatsApp bookings be tracked separately?
Yes. Every appointment is source-tagged for clean, accurate reporting.
Is patient history captured?
Basic operational history is captured now, with structured clinical records coming in later EMR modules.
Does it work for multi-doctor practices?
Yes. It is built for multiple doctors with independent schedules and queues.

Nevic Connect

What is Nevic Connect?
A WhatsApp-native way for patients to book, get reminders, and interact with your clinic in their own language.
Do patients need to install anything?
No. Nevic Connect works entirely inside WhatsApp, which your patients already use every day. There is no app to download and no new account to create.
How is patient consent handled?
A structured consent flow captures and records permission before any WhatsApp engagement begins.
What can patients do over WhatsApp?
Book appointments, receive confirmations and reminders, and get clinic updates in Hindi, English, or Marathi.
Does it reduce no-shows?
Yes. Automated reminders and easy rescheduling help cut missed appointments.
Is it a real chatbot or just broadcasts?
It is an interactive assistant that guides patients through booking and queries, not one-way message blasts.
Can it handle teleconsultation?
WhatsApp OPD teleconsult is supported within the bounds of the Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020.
Who owns the WhatsApp number?
The Healthcare provider. Nevic Connect operates through your clinic's own verified business presence.
What happens to chat data?
It is processed under a data agreement with the healthcare providerand handled in line with DPDP requirements.
Is there a risk if WhatsApp changes its policies?
It is a known channel-dependency risk we actively design around, and core booking remains available through other paths.

Data & Security

Who owns patient data?
The healthcare provider. As Data Fiduciary under the DPDP Act 2023, the healthcare provider owns the data and Nevic Labs acts only as Data Processor.
Is my patient data sold or shared?
Never sold. Sharing is limited to disclosed sub-processors such as Meta/WhatsApp, strictly to deliver the service.
How is data protected in transit and at rest?
TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest, with access controlled by role.
Are you DPDP compliant?
Yes, with a Data Processing Agreement, a versioned consent ledger, and per-field purpose tagging.
Where is data hosted?
On AWS infrastructure, keeping data within India.
How is access controlled?
Role-based access with MFA, so staff see only what their role permits.
Do you keep audit logs?
Yes. Key actions are audit-logged, including acknowledgements on patient alerts.
What about deleted records?
Clinical records use soft-delete with legal hold to meet retention obligations.
What happens in a data breach?
A defined incident-response process governs containment, notification, and remediation.
